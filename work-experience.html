Publicis Health Media

Paid Search Associate

September 2016 — Present

Publicis Health Media is a media agency solely focused on the health and wellness space. It is a part of the Publicis Groupe network, which is headquartered in Paris, France. In the Philadelphia office, I worked as a Paid Search Associate (co-op), working on mostly Janssen brands. My tasks included analyzing SQR and Action Insight Reports to initiate new keyword, campaign, and ad group strategies and eliminate existing low-performing strategies; i.e. adding new negative keywords. I also performed ad copy analysis to discover optimizations to increase on-site engagement and CTR by recommending A/B ad copy or ad title tests to find optimal solutions. I spent a good amount of time building organized dashboards for the brands I work on, using queries pulling from DoubleClick and Google Analytics in Microsoft Excel to ensure quick and efficient monthly and quarterly reporting. Another one of my tasks included creating and uploading bulk sheets for new campaigns, ad groups, keywords, ads, sitelinks, and call-outs for easy upload into DoubleClick. Bi-weekly, I monitored competition to record new competitors entering into our brands’ landscapes and ensured the brands had high positioning and SOV. I used SEM Rush to view the paid keywords competitors were bidding on.

Urban Outfitters, Inc.

Digital Marketing Co-op

September 2015 — March 2016

Urban Outfitters, Inc. is a men and women's fashion and accessories company headquartered in the Navy Yard, Philadelphia. As the Anthropologie Digital Marketing Co-op, I assisted the email marketing team, as well as the SEO and Paid Search teams during my 6 month internship. I was responsible for steps along the lifecycle of Anthropologie's email program including calendar creation, daily reporting and analysis, and competitive monitoring. Lastly, I assisted the SEO and Paid Search team by conducting keyword research using Google AdWords for new and existing products on website, updating title tags, and adding special hours on Google My Business during holiday for Anthropologie stores.
